## Background Information
- Milkers was originally launched on **October 11, 2013**, by **@GuardianAngel** as a private cheat for **Counter-Strike: Global Offensive**. Initially designed as a legit-only tool, it was never intended for public release or sale. Over time, rage features were introduced after the cheat proved undetected—these were quickly flagged in matchmaking, and the cheat itself got detected..

- In **2016**, development stopped, later resuming in **2018** when **Counter-Strike: Global Offensive** went free-to-play. During this period, **@GuardianAngel** privately compiled shared `.dll` files with friends. Those users encouraged a public release, leading to a website and an invite-based system. How ever invites were not disrubuted as most people did not wan't the cheats detection rates to skyrocket.

- By **2023**, following the announcement of **Counter-Strike 2**, development focus shifted toward **Roblox** after its new anti-cheat system was released. Early **Roblox** builds were unstable, but after extensive testing and iteration, a stable build was completed in **late 2024**. Licenses were distributed to trusted users, and development continued for both **Roblox** and **Counter-Strike 2** with contributions from additional developers.

- In **2025**, the Milkers domain was suspended. Operations continued through a Discord server managed by a custom bot. After the server was eventually terminated, the website was restored and development resumed as normal.
