## Background Information

- **Nihon** was started by **@Immune** and several other developers in **October 2020**. In its early stages, it received little traction and remained a smaller alternative within the exploit community. At the time, larger executors such as **KRNL**, **Fluxus**, and **Oxygen U** dominated the space. Despite this, **Nihon** gained recognition for its polished interface and solid execution strength, powered by its own custom solution. A similar situation occurred with **Electron**, which later rebranded into **Volcano** and eventually became successful.

- Following the introduction of Hyperion, **Nihon** was briefly released on the UWP (Microsoft Store) version of Roblox. On **August 28, 2024**, a global banwave targeted UWP executors. By **September 2024**, UWP **Nihon** was patched, alongside every other UWP-based executor. For a short period, downgrading the UWP client remained a workaround, but this method only functioned for a few weeks before becoming unusable.

- In **early 2024**, development activity increased significantly, leading to an official release on **October 24, 2024**, where **Nihon** delivered its strongest and most refined performance period. However, on **February 3, 2025**, the team split following internal conflict. The owner stated that a former administrator and **Sero** had nuked the Discord server and banned users, while clarifying that **Zenith** was not involved and had returned the server vanity URL. No further public explanation was given.

- After this incident, **Nihon** became largely inactive aside from smaller releases. On **January 11, 2026**, **Nihon** was officially discontinued. Shortly after, on **January 13, 2026**, the project rebranded to **Isaeva**, which was officially released on **February 22, 2026**.
